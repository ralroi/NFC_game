How to Use the NFC Scanner

1
Connect RC522

Ensure the RC522 reader is wired to the Pi GPIO. SPI must be enabled in raspi-config.

2
Click "Start Scan"

The reader listens for 10 seconds. Hold a card near the reader.

3
Card detected

The UID appears and existing registration info loads automatically.

4
Take action

Select a faction and register, or use reset/block as needed.

Card types: NTAG213/215/216 and MIFARE Classic are supported game cards. Bank cards (ISO 14443-4) are detected and logged but cannot be registered. The scanner identifies the card type automatically.
